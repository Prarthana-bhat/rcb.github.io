ROYAL CHALLENGERS BENGALURU

The Royal Challengers Bangalore (often abbreviated as RCB) are a franchise cricket team based in Bangalore, Karnataka, that plays in the Indian Premier League (IPL). It was founded in 2008 by United Spirits and named after the company's liquor brand Royal Challenge. Since its inception, the team has played its home matches at the M. Chinnaswamy Stadium.

FANS

The Royal Challengers have a huge and passionate fan base all over India and especially in the city of Bangalore. The fans, known to be loyal and vocal in their support,[25] often turn up in large numbers for RCB's home matches turning the stadium into what is called a "sea of red".[26][27][28] They are well known for their chants of "R-C-B, R-C-B"[29][30] and the co-ordinated Mexican wave at the Chinnaswamy.[31] The stadium organisers also provide the home team fans with cheer kits, RCB flags and noisemakers among other items.[32] Royal Challengers Bangalore have formed a fan-following group named as Bold Army.
